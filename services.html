Registered office

Registered office

According to the Company Ordinance Cap. 622, a company must have a registered office in Hong Kong to which all communications and notices may be addressed. Rent for an office in Hong Kong is expensive so our Registered address services is a cost saving alternative.

Annual Audit

Annual Audit

According to the Company Ordinance, all limited companies must prepare the audited financial statements together with the directors’ report every year. The auditing must be conducted by a Certified Public Accountant (Practicing) Firm. The audited financial statements should be ready before the Annual General Meeting.

Taxation Service

Taxation Service

The Inland Revenue Department (the “IRD”) will send the first Profits Tax Return – Corporation in around 18 months after incorporation. The Company needs to fill in the tax return within 3 months and submit together with the audited financial statements and a tax computation. An assessment will be issued by the IRD if there is assessable profit. The company needs to pay profits tax according to the tax assessment. Hereafter the profits tax return will be issued on the first working day of April every year. The original tax deadline would be within 1 month as mentioned on the tax return form. However, through appointing us as the tax representative of your Company, we can apply for extension on behalf of you. The extended deadline for submitting the tax return will depend on the year ended date of the Company. The profits tax payable depends on the net assessable profit of the Company. The Tax rate for the first $2,000,000 assessable profit would be 8.25% and the remaining would be 16.5%.
